B.Tech ME 2014-19

Hello everyone, I am Ujjwal; I am a level IV Engineer at Anglo-Eastern PVT ltd. I am an alumnus of K.R. Mangalam University. I graduated in 2019. I chose B.Tech ME as I was passionate about the subjects this course offered. I was already aware of the jobs offered after this degree. When I came to KRMU, I realized that Mechanical Engineering is such a vast topic that the opportunities are endless. I give credits to my professors & mentors as they never gave up on me even when I was confused & curious. With the help of management & my sheer will to become what I dreamed of years ago, it turned into reality. I believe that K.R. Mangalam university was one of the best decisions of my life.
